# Mini Quiz: Chapter 03

Test your knowledge of the concepts covered in Chapter 03: Actuators and Mechanisms. Choose the best answer for each question.

---

1.  Which type of electric motor requires an external Electronic Speed Controller (ESC) for commutation?
    a) DC Motor
    b) **BLDC Motor**
    c) Stepper Motor
    d) Servo Motor

2.  A hydraulic system is best characterized by its use of:
    a) Compressed air for fast responses.
    b) **Incompressible fluid for high force and stiffness.**
    c) Electromagnetic fields for precise stepping.
    d) Brushes and a commutator for simple control.

3.  What is the primary advantage of a stepper motor for certain robotic applications?
    a) Very high speed at low torque.
    b) Continuous rotation without discrete steps.
    c) **Precise positional control in open-loop systems.**
    d) High efficiency for continuous operation.

4.  If a motor produces 100 RPM and a gearbox with a gear ratio of 4:1 (ideal) is attached, what will be the output speed?
    a) 400 RPM
    b) **25 RPM**
    c) 100 RPM
    d) 4 RPM

5.  "Backlash" in a gear train refers to:
    a) The maximum torque a gearbox can withstand.
    b) The efficiency of the gearbox.
    c) **Lost motion or slack between meshing gears.**
    d) The speed at which gears can rotate.

---

**Answer Key:**
1.  b
2.  b
3.  c
4.  b
5.  c
