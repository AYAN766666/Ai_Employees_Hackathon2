# Mini Quiz: Chapter 01

Test your knowledge of the concepts covered in Chapter 01: Introduction to Physical AI. Choose the best answer for each question.

---

1.  What is the primary characteristic that distinguishes Physical AI from traditional Artificial Intelligence?
    a) Its use of advanced neural networks.
    b) Its focus on data analysis and prediction.
    c) **Its interaction with the real world through a physical body.**
    d) Its reliance on cloud computing resources.

2.  The concept of "embodiment" in Physical AI suggests that:
    a) AI should be invisible and run in the background.
    b) **An agent's intelligence is shaped by its physical form and sensory-motor capabilities.**
    c) All AI must be controlled by human operators.
    d) AI systems must be purely software-based.

3.  Which of the following sensors would primarily be classified as a proprioceptive sensor?
    a) Lidar sensor on an autonomous car.
    b) A camera used for object recognition.
    c) **An encoder measuring a robot arm's joint angle.**
    d) An ultrasonic sensor for obstacle detection.

4.  In the context of humanoid balancing, what is the Zero Moment Point (ZMP)?
    a) The exact center of the robot's physical body.
    b) **A theoretical point on the ground where the sum of all moments due to gravity and inertial forces is zero.**
    c) The point where the robot's battery is located.
    d) The starting position for any robot movement.

5.  Which balancing strategy involves a humanoid robot taking a step to regain stability when facing very large or sustained disturbances?
    a) Ankle Strategy
    b) Hip Strategy
    c) **Step Strategy**
    d) Torso Strategy

---

**Answer Key:**
1.  c
2.  b
3.  c
4.  b
5.  c
