# Chapter 12: Introduction to Digital Twins

Digital Twins are virtual replicas of physical systems, and they hold immense potential for robotics. This chapter introduces the concept of Digital Twins, their architecture, and how they can be used to monitor, analyze, and optimize robotic systems in real-time. We will discuss the benefits of Digital Twins for predictive maintenance, fault detection, and system design.
