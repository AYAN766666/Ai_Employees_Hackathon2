# Mini Quiz: Chapter 14

Test your knowledge of the concepts covered in Chapter 14: Ethical and Societal Implications of Physical AI. Choose the best answer for each question.

---

1.  Which ethical principle requires clear lines of responsibility for the actions and impacts of AI systems?
    a) Beneficence
    b) Transparency
    c) Fairness
    d) **Accountability**

2.  What is the ethical principle that states AI should be designed to do good and contribute positively to human well-being?
    a) Non-maleficence
    b) Accountability
    c) **Beneficence**
    d) Privacy

3.  The impact of AI-driven automation on employment typically involves both job displacement and:
    a) A complete halt to technological progress.
    b) A decrease in overall productivity.
    c) **Job creation and job augmentation.**
    d) An elimination of the need for human skills.

4.  When robots equipped with extensive sensors collect vast amounts of data about their surroundings and the humans they interact with, this primarily raises concerns about:
    a) Actuator efficiency.
    b) Kinematic complexity.
    c) **Privacy and Surveillance.**
    d) Control system stability.

5.  What is "Privacy by Design" in the context of AI ethics?
    a) Designing robots to be physically small to avoid detection.
    b) **Integrating privacy protections into the design and development of AI systems from the outset.**
    c) Allowing users to opt-out of all data collection.
    d) Using only open-source software for AI development.

---

**Answer Key:**
1.  d
2.  c
3.  c
4.  c
5.  b
