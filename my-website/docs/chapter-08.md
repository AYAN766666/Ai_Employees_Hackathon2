# Chapter 08: Human-Robot Interaction (HRI)

Welcome to Chapter 08 of "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics"! This chapter explores Human-Robot Interaction (HRI), the study of how humans and robots can effectively and safely work together. As robots become more prevalent in our daily lives, from industrial settings to homes and public spaces, designing intuitive, natural, and trustworthy interactions becomes paramount. We will delve into the principles, challenges, and key aspects of creating seamless collaborative experiences between humans and intelligent physical agents.
