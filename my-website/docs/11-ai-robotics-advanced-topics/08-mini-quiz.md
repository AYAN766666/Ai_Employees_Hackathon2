# Mini Quiz: Chapter 11

Test your knowledge of the concepts covered in Chapter 11: Artificial Intelligence in Robotics: Advanced Topics. Choose the best answer for each question.

---

1.  What is the primary objective of "Cognitive Robotics"?
    a) To enhance the robot's physical strength and speed.
    b) To solely focus on improving sensor data acquisition.
    c) **To develop robots capable of human-like intelligence, including reasoning, learning, and planning.**
    d) To minimize the robot's computational resources.

2.  A map that combines geometric information about an environment with semantic labels (e.g., "this is a kitchen," "this is a chair") is known as a:
    a) Occupancy Grid Map
    b) Feature Map
    c) **Semantic Map**
    d) Topological Map

3.  Which of the following is NOT a typical advantage of Multi-Robot Systems (MRS) over a single robot?
    a) Increased coverage.
    b) Enhanced robustness and redundancy.
    c) Improved scalability.
    d) **Simplified individual robot design and autonomy.**

4.  In the context of Multi-Robot Systems, what is a key characteristic of "Swarm Intelligence"?
    a) A single, powerful central controller managing all robots.
    b) **Complex global behaviors emerging from simple local interactions between robots.**
    c) Robots always require explicit, predefined global plans.
    d) Individual robots are highly intelligent and complex.

5.  What is another common term for "Learning from Demonstration (LfD)"?
    a) Reinforcement Learning
    b) Supervised Learning
    c) **Imitation Learning**
    d) Unsupervised Learning

---

**Answer Key:**
1.  c
2.  c
3.  d
4.  b
5.  c
