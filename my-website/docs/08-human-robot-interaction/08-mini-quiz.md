# Mini Quiz: Chapter 08

Test your knowledge of the concepts covered in Chapter 08: Human-Robot Interaction (HRI). Choose the best answer for each question.

---

1.  What does the acronym HRI stand for?
    a) Human-Robot Integration
    b) High-Resolution Imaging
    c) **Human-Robot Interaction**
    d) Home Robot Intelligence

2.  Which of the following is an example of non-verbal communication from a robot to a human?
    a) The robot speaking a command.
    b) The human physically guiding the robot's arm.
    c) **The robot using LED lights to indicate its status.**
    d) The human typing a command on a GUI.

3.  What is the primary purpose of an Emergency Stop (E-Stop) mechanism in collaborative robotics?
    a) To save the robot's battery life.
    b) To signal the robot to continue working.
    c) **To immediately halt all robot motion and power in an emergency.**
    d) To communicate robot intentions to humans.

4.  When humans place too much trust in an autonomous robot, leading to potential over-reliance and unsafe situations, this is known as:
    a) Under-trust
    b) Appropriate trust
    c) **Over-trust**
    d) Misguided trust

5.  Which design principle in HRI ensures that a robot's actions are understandable and avoids sudden or unexpected movements, contributing to building trust?
    a) Adaptability
    b) Empathy
    c) **Predictability and Transparency**
    d) Social Intelligence
